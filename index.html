Join thousands at the Great Northern Viking Festival — the Midwest's premier celebration of Norse culture and Viking heritage. Held in Mankato, Minnesota on June 13–14, 2026, this immersive two-day event brings the Viking Age to life through combat, craft, food, and storytelling.

Festival Highlights

⚔️ Battle Arena

Full-contact Viking combat using historically accurate weapons and armor. Watch warriors clash in organized melees and single combat tournaments.

🏘️ Viking Village

Walk through an authentic Norse settlement. See blacksmiths forge steel, weavers create textiles, and woodcarvers shape intricate designs.

🍺 Mead Hall

Sample traditional mead, ale, and Viking-era cuisine. From spit-roasted meats to hearth bread, feast like a Norse chieftain.

📜 Saga Circle

Expert storytellers recount the great Norse sagas. Learn about runes, mythology, and the real history behind the legends.

🎪 Market Square

Over 50 artisan vendors offering handcrafted Viking jewelry, leather armor, pottery, and one-of-a-kind Norse-inspired art.

👧 Children's Realm

Mini Viking training, shield painting, treasure hunts, and costume dress-up stations make this an unforgettable family adventure.

About the Festival

The Great Northern Viking Festival celebrates the Scandinavian heritage that shaped Minnesota's culture and communities. Founded to preserve and share Norse traditions, the festival brings together historians, reenactors, artisans, and families from across the country. Every element — from the hand-forged weapons to the recipes passed down through generations — is designed to offer an authentic glimpse into the Viking world.
